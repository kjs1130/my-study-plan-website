About Our Service

What is My Study Plan?

My Study Plan is a web service that helps you achieve your learning goals effectively. It provides tools for creating study plans, tracking progress, and maintaining a focused learning environment.

Key Features

Study Plan Management

Set goals and timelines, create detailed plans, and maintain a systematic study routine.

D-Day Counter

Track and manage the time remaining until important exams or target dates.

Study Time Tracker

Measure and record your actual study time using the stopwatch feature.

Focus Sounds

Create a conducive learning environment with various ambient sounds:

  • Rain Sounds
  • Fireplace Crackling
  • Mountain Birds

Cafe Music

Enhance your study sessions with various cafe music options:

  • Lo-fi Cafe Music
  • Classical Cafe Music
  • Jazz Cafe Music

Motivational Quotes

Stay motivated with daily inspirational quotes to boost your learning enthusiasm.

How to Use

  1. Click 'Create Plan' on the homepage to start a new study plan.
  2. Set up your D-Day to track time until your target date.
  3. Use the stopwatch to measure your actual study time.
  4. Play ambient sounds or cafe music when you need to focus.
  5. Check your overall progress in the 'My Plans' section.
